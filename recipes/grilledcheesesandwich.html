Grilled cheese sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (white, wheat, sourdough, or your favorite kind)
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2-3 slices of cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, Gruyere, Swiss, or any combination you like)
  • Optional: pinch of salt and pepper, sliced tomato, avocado, thinly sliced onion, herbs

Instructions:

  1. Spread one tablespoon of butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  3. Place one slice of bread, butter-side down, in the skillet.
  4. Top with cheese and any other desired ingredients.
  5. Place the other slice of bread, butter-side up, on top of the cheese.
  6. Cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  7. Cut in half and enjoy!

Tips:

  • For a richer flavor, use mayonnaise instead of butter on the outside of the bread.
  • To prevent the bread from burning, cook over medium heat and don't press down on the sandwich too hard.
  • If you like your cheese extra melty, you can cover the skillet with a lid while the sandwich cooks.
  • Get creative with your ingredients! Try adding different types of cheese, meats, vegetables, or herbs.

Classic Maggi Masala Noodles:

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet Maggi noodles (Masala flavor)
  • 1 cup waterr
  • 1/2 teaspoon oil
  • Masala packet from the Maggi noodles

Instructions:

  1. Boil the water in a pan or saucepan.
  2. Add the Maggi noodles and masala packet.
  3. Add oil and mix well.
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until the noodles are cooked through.
  5. Add chopped vegetables if desired.
  6. Cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

Tips:

  • You can adjust the amount of water depending on how soupy you like your Maggi.
  • Feel free to experiment with different vegetables, spices, or even meats to customize your Maggi to your taste.
  • For a richer flavor, you can use butter or ghee instead of oil.
  • If you're short on time, you can microwave your Maggi. Simply add all the ingredients to a microwave-safe bowl and microwave for 2-3 minutes on high power.

I hope this helps you make your favorite Maggi recipe!

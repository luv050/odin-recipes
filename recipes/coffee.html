Using instant coffee:

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 teaspoons instant coffee granules
  • 1/2 cup hot water (around 90-95Â°C)
  • 1/2 cup milk (whole, skim, almond, oat, etc., to your preference)
  • Sweetener (optional, sugar, honey, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Pour the hot water into a mug.
  2. Add the instant coffee granules and stir until dissolved.
  3. Add oil and mix well.
  4. Warm the milk
  5. Pour the warm milk into the coffee and stir gently.
  6. Add sweetener to taste, if using.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

Tips:

  • Adjust the coffee-to-milk ratio to your taste preference. Some people prefer a stronger coffee flavor, while others prefer a more balanced mix.
  • Experiment with different types of milk to find your favorite flavor combination
  • For a richer flavor, use full-fat milk or add a teaspoon of cream or butter.
  • If you like your coffee hot, preheat your mug before adding the coffee.
  • Enjoy your delicious normal milk coffee!
